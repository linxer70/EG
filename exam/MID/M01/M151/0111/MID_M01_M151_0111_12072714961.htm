다음 중 대화의 흐름이 자연스럽지 못한 것을 고르시오.
①  A : When shall we meet?
B : How about this Friday?
②  A : Someone dropped a bucket on me.
B : That sounds bad.
③  A : What are you going to go there?
B : I can go tomorrow afternoon.
④  A : What were they doing?
B : They were carrying a bucket of water.
⑤  A : Why are you late today?
B : I had to exercise in the morning.

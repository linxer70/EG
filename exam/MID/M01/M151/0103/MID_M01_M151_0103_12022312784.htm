다음 두 사람의 대화가 자연스럽지 못한 것을 고르시오.
①  A : I feel terrible today. My throat really hurts. I have a sore throat.
B : That sounds bad.
②  A : We won the game. It was nine to six.
B : That's great.
③  A : My grandfather's coming home today.
B : That sounds bad.
A : Yes, I am happy to see them.
④  A : I have a toothache.
B : Oh, that's too bad.
⑤  A : I lost my bike.
B : I'm sorry to hear that.

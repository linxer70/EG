밑줄 친 부분을 다른 말로 바꿔 쓸 때 의미가 다른 것은?
①  A : She didn't count out your change for you.
B : What do you mean? (=I don't understand.)
②  A : Subtract 4600 from 5000 won.
B : That leaves 400 won. (=That change is 400 won.)
③  A : What time do you have?
B : It's a quarter to nine. (=It's nine forty five.)
④  A : I think soccer is fun. Do you agree?
B : Yes, I agree. (=Yes, I think so.)
⑤  A : The earth is round.
B : You can say that again. (=You're right.)

다음 중 문장을 잘못 바꾼 것은?
①  When do you do your homework?
→ Please tell me when you do your homework.
②  Mike is faster than any other boy in his class.
→ Mike is the fastest boy in his class.
③  Where do you live?
→ Please tell me where you live.
④  What does your best friend do after school?
→ Do you know what your best friend do after school?
⑤  No other boy in his class is as slow as John.
→ In his class, John is the slowest of all the boys.

다음 주어진 문장을 바꾸어 쓸 때 서로 의미가 다르거나 어법상 잘못 고쳐진 것은?
①  If married to him, she will live happily.
= If she is married to him, she will live happily.
②  Fortunately, he won the first prize.
= It's fortunate that he won the first prize.
③  Unless you join us, we will not go hiking.
= If you don't join us, we will not go hiking.
④  John met his wife at Lincoln College in 1915.
= It was John who met his wife at Lincoln college in 1915.
⑤  I wish I could travel around the world.
= I am sorry that I couldn't travel around the world.

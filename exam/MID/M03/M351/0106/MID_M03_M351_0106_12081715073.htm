다음 중 한국 문화에서 받아들일 수 없는 행동을 모두 고르시오.
①  People often kiss friends on the cheek when they meet.
②  People always arrive on time when they're invited to someone's home.
③  People call seniors over with their palms up.
④  It's OK to ask for a second helping when people are eating a friend's house.
⑤  When friends have dinner out together, each person pays his or her share.

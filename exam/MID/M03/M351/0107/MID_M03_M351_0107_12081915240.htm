다음 중 밑줄 친 곳에 생략된 말이 바르게 연결된 것은?
①  When _______ staying in London, I met Tom. - (I am)
②  Though _______ tired, I went on working. - (you are)
③  If _______ badly chosen, friends will not be good to us. - (they be)
④  When_______ planted in a flower garden, it becomes a flower. - (it is)
⑤  The flowers can be eaten if _______ picked while they are still buds. - (they were)

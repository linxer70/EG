다음 두 문장의 의미가 같지 않은 것은?
①  Not everybody in the global village learns to read and write.
= Everybody in the global village doesn't learn to read and write.
②  Not everyone likes you.
= Everyone doesn't like you.
③  Ms. Simpson does not always smile at me.
= Ms. Simpson never smiles at me.
④   I don't like all the books that you gave.
= I don't like some books that you gave.
⑤   Both of us can't take a rest.
= One of us can take a rest.

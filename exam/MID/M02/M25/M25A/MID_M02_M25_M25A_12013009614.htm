다음 중 짝지어진 두 문장의 의미가 서로 다른 것은?
①  She likes to collect stamps.
= She likes collecting stamps.
②  She tried to keep a diary in English.
= She tried keeping a diary in English.
③   It started to rain.
= It started raining.
④  They began to look for a boat.
= They began looking for a boat.
⑤  He hates swimming in the river.
= He hates to swim in the river.

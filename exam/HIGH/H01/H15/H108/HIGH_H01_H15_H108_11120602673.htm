The ruins of Delphi are an important archaeological site in Greece. In ancient times, Delphi was considered the place where Heaven and Earth met. In the center of Delphi was the temple of the Greek God Apollo. Kings and common people came from all over to get advice and have their future (B)tell by the Oracle of Apollo, a spiritual woman who lived there. Delphi was also famous for its Pythian sports games. They were held every four years, just like the Olympics of today.

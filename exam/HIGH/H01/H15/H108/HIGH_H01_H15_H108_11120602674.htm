Iguazu National Park is located on the northern border of Argentina. The park includes one of the most spectacular natural wonders in South America: Iguazu Falls. Taller than Niagara Falls and twice as wide, Iguazu Falls consists of 270 waterfalls spread out over 2.7 kilometers along the Iguazu River. The surrounding jungle has more than 2,000 species of plants and is home to diverse wildlife. Across the river lies Brazil, so visitors can also enjoy Iguazu Falls from the Brazilian side.
